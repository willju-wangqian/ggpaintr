Notes on hidden-tab pickers

A first probe after switching pick_ds while the "Plot controls" outer tab was active read the aes pickers as refreshed (diamonds, 10 cols) but the stage pickers and colvars as stale (mpg, 11 cols). The state corrected itself once the "Data inputs" tab was brought back into view, with every picker reading the new 10 diamonds columns. This is the standard Shiny suspended-output behavior — renderUI outputs in a hidden tab don't tick over until visibility resumes — and not a runtime bug. The shape is the same as the BUG-4 / BUG-A1 probe artifacts the project has already filed twice (see dev/audit/feature-coverage-2026-05-13-resolution.md); recorded here so future audits read pickers in their natural tab and don't refile.
