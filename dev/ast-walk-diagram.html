ggpaintr — how the AST walk works

A ggpaintr formula is parsed into a typed AST of ptr_* nodes. Several walks over that tree turn it into Shiny widgets, then (on every "Update plot") into a finished ggplot + the code-panel text. Click the lifecycle steps, click any tree node, and pick a walker to animate its traversal.

3 · The walks pick one, then "Animate traversal"

The traversal narration appears here. For the transform walks it follows the structural recursion (root→layers, layer→data_arg+children, pipeline→stages, call→args) and shows what the walk does at each node. The upstream pointer on a var node is deliberately NOT followed — that rule lives once, in ptr_walk().
